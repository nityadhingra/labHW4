Avocado Roll

The simple but delicious avocado roll! Satisfy your sushi cravings with the freshest of ingredients. The creamy avocado will melt in your mouth, padded with sushi rice and nori ofcourse! Pair it with soy sauce and pickled ginger for the best experience. Miso soup on the side is also great, a classic combination of flavors:)
